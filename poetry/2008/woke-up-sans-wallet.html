Woke up without my wallet

Finally found myself
             a place where
“words are transformative”
again.

For a time we forgot all those
dead ones, that in general
are a good idea to
keep in your back pocket.

With our other friends
the distance had made the game for keeps;
meanwhile, we tried
each other’s eyes
as in a mirror.

As night became just more day
each breath had me wondering
             high up as we were
if I could breathe a little less,
make it the last.
