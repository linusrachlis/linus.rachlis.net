Status Update (or, Please note the compression artifacts)

Linus
wants you to know that
even though my identity sits here distilled
like some computer file in a directory listing
name, thumbnail, last modified, last accessed, file size
randomly accessible from anywhere anytime
I am a particular person.

If you run into me in the street, it is still special
even if the street is not.
True, you can invite me to your party with a click
and I may even come
but might feel awkward
and unsure if I’m really wanted
and you can’t beat the spontaneity of
hey
we’re here now
fancy some coffee?

My profile is in roughly the same format as everyone else’s
the only differences being the way I coloured it in,
letting you ignore the fact
that I was created from the ground up—
I am an improvised person.

I am more of a person than some, and less than others
Smarter than some, duller than others
My heart is bigger than some, and smaller than others’
My angst is not a point on an emotigraph
or cause for a status update
It’s the meaning of my life
balancing on a question

You can message me without getting in touch with me
although hey, messages are nice, while
I tell myself I can shapeshift
into just a face in a book

for now, the consummate utilitarian
until taking flight into the future
when I’m ready

My wings are broader than some, and frailer than others
though we all wait to take flight.
