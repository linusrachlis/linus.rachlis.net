Precipitation, Sublimation

Your folks
your friends
even your foes
all saw it before you did:
the first time you fell into a conversation
the first time you rose to an occasion,

not exactly
changing,
realising after the fact
how few first times were left,
how they were all gathering skyward,
somewhere far-off and safe,
not exactly
changing.

So pick an attitude
take some strides.

These mornings will fall
while afternoons rise

—the bottom of a pail is broken through—

wow
             that’s bright blue.

Looks good on
                          you.
