Things that are red & good

With icy ears
your head leans
heavy against the bricks.

Vision afloat
cider in hand
the hour’s shovelling
a warm chest weight;
the Christmas lights need taking down.

Cranberries in the fireflicker that night,
their dried wrinkles between your fingers as the blood returns.

In the morning
signs of yesterday
loosening embers that still burn.
